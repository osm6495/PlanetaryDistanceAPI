Thank You for Using the Planetary Distance Calculator!

Please provide an origin and destination query parameter to get started. You can use any of these planets:

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune

Example: /distance?origin=Earth&destination=Mars

The distance is the average distance in miles between the two planets, since the distances vary depending on where the planets are in their orbits.

Distances courtesy of theplanets.org.
